Welcome. This blog documents the development of **PS-TESTER**, a thrust-stand test system for UAV propulsion.

##### The PS-TESTER Project

PS-TESTER is a **propeller-motor thrust test stand** for characterizing, testing, and designing the propulsion systems of unmanned aerial vehicles (drones). It quantitatively measures the performance of the propulsion chain — motor, propeller, ESC, and battery — so that reliable data can be obtained during the design phase.

Key measurements / features:

- **Thrust** and **torque** measurement
- Simultaneous logging of **RPM**, **voltage**, **current**, and **power**
- Power efficiency analysis (thrust per watt, g/W)
- Data logging and visualization

> Detailed specifications, setups, and results are covered in individual blog posts.
